Who We Are

A Decade of Premium Real Estate

KingMac of Property was founded on a simple belief — that every Nigerian deserves access to exceptional real estate. With over a decade of experience spanning Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt and beyond, we have built a reputation for integrity, expertise, and results that speak for themselves.

From luxury penthouses in Ikoyi to prime commercial developments in Victoria Island, our portfolio reflects the full breadth of Nigeria's thriving property market. We serve discerning buyers, astute investors, and the Nigerian diaspora who trust us to manage their most important investments.
